Original Instructions.
The information contained in this manual is current at time of printing. Improvements to the machine are ongoing and may not be covered; in these cases, contact your Tigercat-TCi Dealer for the appropriate information.
WARNINGS must mention the HAZARD (source of the harm, for example rotating drive belt), the CONSEQUENCES of exposure to the hazard (harm that could occur, for example entanglement or burns), and INSTRUCTIONS on how to AVOID the harm (do not remove guards, do not approach engine while running, etc).Breathing diesel engine exhaust exposes you to chemicals known to the State of California to cause cancer and birth defects or other reproductive harm.
Always start and operate the engine in a well-ventilated area.
If in an enclosed area, vent the exhaust to the outside.
Do not modify or tamper with the exhaust system.
Do not idle the engine except as necessary.
For more information, go to www.P65warnings.ca.gov/diesel
