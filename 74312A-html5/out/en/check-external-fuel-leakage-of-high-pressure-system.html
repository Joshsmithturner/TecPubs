Check External Fuel Leakage Of High-Pressure System

Document ID: 5145 | Version: 2.4 | Last Modified Date: Sep 3, 2024

Preparation:

  • Access the top part of the engine.

Procedure:

  1. Check for fuel leakage at the fuel rail and injectors.

    Location of Injector High-Pressure Inlets (6-Cylinder Shown)

    A

    Fuel Rail

    B

    Injector High-Pressure Inlets

    C

    High-Pressure Fuel Line from High-Pressure Pump to Fuel Rail

    • Check for odours to identify any fuel contamination or leakage.

    • Visually check for any fuel damage, contamination, or leakage on the fuel rail and in the injector high-pressure inlets.

    1. If there is an odour of diesel fuel or signs of fuel at the high-pressure connectors, locate and correct the damage or leaks, then use the PT-Box tool or LogOn to clear all fault codes and verify that the fault is resolved.

    2. If there is no odour of fuel, continue to the next step.

  2. The fuel high-pressure system works properly.
