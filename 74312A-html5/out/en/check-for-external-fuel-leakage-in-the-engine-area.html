Note

Not all inlet and outlet ports on the pre-filter are used. Inlet and outlet connections depend on the machine configuration.

Preparation:

  • Run the engine until it reaches normal operating temperature.

Procedure:

  1. Check for visible external fuel leakage in the engine area.

    • Check for fuel leakage at the low-pressure hose connectors.

    • Check the fuel filter connectors.

    • Check the high-pressure pump connectors.

    • Check the fuel metering valve (MPROP) connectors.

    • Check the fuel hoses for kinks.

    1. If external fuel leakage is visible in the engine area, investigate these possible root causes:

      • Connectors are loose or worn

      • Hoses are damaged

      Locate and correct the cause of the leakage, then use the PT-Box tool or LogOn to clear all fault codes and verify that the fault is resolved.

    2. If no external fuel leakage is visible in the engine area, continue to the next step.

  2. The fuel system works properly.
