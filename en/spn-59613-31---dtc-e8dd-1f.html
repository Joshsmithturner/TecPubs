SPN-59613-31 / DTC-E8DD-1F

ECU Internal: Diagnosis of Lead Torque Limitation (for Fuel Pressure Control) Forced by ECU Monitoring Level 2

Document ID: 6020 | Version: 2.1 | Last Modified Date: Oct 29, 2024

Fault Description: 

The electronic control unit (ECU) determines engine torque on the basis of the requests of the various vehicle functions, which are in some cases additive, in some cases as maximum, and in some cases as limitation. The total engine torque requested is compared with the maximum permitted engine torque. Fuel pressure is based on the requested torque. Therefore the torque, known as 'main torque', is calculated separately. If this fault occurs, the ECU has detected that the value of the requested main torque is higher than the maximum permitted torque.

Note

If this fault appears, the ECU has a fatal error or an incorrect data set calibration. Contact the Tigercat Industries Service Department for more help.

Solution:

  • Contact the Tigercat Industries Service Department for more help.
