Oil Leaking From Turbine Outlet

Document ID: 27367 | Version: 2.11 | Last Modified Date: Sep 6, 2024

  1. Record and save the following data

    • Machine serial number

    • Machine hours

    • Clone with logs (IQAN)

    • Data stored (PT-Box)

    • Fault codes (PT-Box)

  2. Check the exhaust manifold for oil leakage. Remove the manifold to inspect if all ports are dry. If any oil leakage is detected, contact the Tigercat Industries Service Department.

  3. Check the intake manifold for oil build-up. If the intake manifold shows a high level of oil build-up, send pictures to the Tigercat Industries Service Department.
