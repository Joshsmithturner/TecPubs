SPN-28950-31 / DTC-7116-1F

Short Circuit in Injection Bank 1 (All Injectors of the Same Bank Can Be Affected)

Document ID: 3909 | Version: 3.1 | Last Modified Date: Sep 10, 2025

Fault Description: 

Hardware errors in the injectors and the respective electronic control unit (ECU) power stages are examined by the ECU. A diagnostics procedure uses pattern detection to identify specific errors and trigger appropriate substitute reactions. When the expected combination of errors is detected, this fault occurs.

Cause: 

The ECU has determined that a short circuit condition exists in injector bank 1.

Possible Failure Modes:

  1. Faulty bank 1 injector circuit wiring, short circuit.

  2. Faulty bank 1 injector solenoid windings, short circuit.

Solution:

  • Check the status of the following related faults:

    SPN-25126-31 / DTC-6226-1F

    Fuel Injector, Firing Order 1: Short Circuit

    SPN-25638-31 / DTC-6426-1F

    Fuel Injector, Firing Order 3: Short Circuit

    SPN-26150-31 / DTC-6626-1F

    Fuel Injector, Firing Order 5: Short Circuit

    1. If any of the listed faults are active, resolve them first and then determine if this fault is also resolved.

    2. If none of the listed faults are active, contact the Tigercat Industries Service Department for more help.
